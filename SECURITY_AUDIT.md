## 3. Vulnerability Analysis & Findings

### [High Severity] Fixed: BetRouter Refund Logic Failure
- **Description**: The `refundPendingBet` function attempted to transfer BNB from the Router balance. However, the protocol correctly transfers the net bet to the underlying `HouseVault` upon placement. This caused all manual refund requests to fail due to "Insufficient Balance" in the Router.
- **Impact**: In the event of an Oracle delay or failure, users would be unable to reclaim their funds via the authorized refund process.
- **Resolution**: Implemented a "Pull-Refund" pattern. The Router now pulls the funds back from the `HouseVault` before executing the user refund.

### [Info] Verified: Reentrancy Protection
- **Observation**: `FunStaking` and `HouseVault` use `ReentrancyGuard`.
- **Test**: A malicious contract `MaliciousStaker` attempted to call `getReward()` recursively.
- **Result**: Successfully blocked by the protocol.

### [Info] Verified: Circuit Breaker (Max Bet Ratio)
- **Observation**: GLP and Vaults have a configurable `maxBetRatio`.
- **Test**: Attempted to place a 100 BNB bet on a vault with 10 BNB liquidity.
- **Result**: Correctly reverted, preventing catastrophic over-exposure of the pool.
